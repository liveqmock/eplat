第二步ABCD步骤
重点：进步判断和治疗。
Airway:
Place advanced airway.
Verify correct position by primary and secondary tube confirmation techniques.
Secure. Continue to monitor tube position throughout the resuscitative effort.
Breathing:
•Supply positive-pressure ventilation with a bag mask or appropriate mechanical ventilation.
•Verify bilateral chest movement.
•Check blood oxygenation with a pulse oximeter; order arterial blood gas analyses.
•Arrange for mechanical ventilation (unless patient resumes spontaneous respirations).
•Check for potential complications caused by resuscitation, such as pneumothorax, rib fractures, sternal fractures, and improper airway placement.
•Proper airway management and adequate ventilation are critical in any cardiac arrest.
•Conditions to rule out can be recalled with the mnemonic DOPE: Displacement of tube, Obstruction of tube, Pneumothorax, or Equipment failure.
Circulation:
•Confirm that the “flat line” is not a result of operator error, which is a more common cause of a flat ECG than fine VF. (Note: One good clue to the possibility of operator error is when “asystolic” patients have a pulse.) Be sure to ensure proper connection of monitor cable to patient.
Verify proper connection of monitor cable to defibrillator.
Ensure that the power switch is ON.

Memory Aids for the Causes of Asystole: use the same memory aids as you used for PEA. (See
