第一阶段 ABCD
u重点:基本 CPR和除颤
uA : 开放气道
uB : 检查呼吸，进行人工呼吸
uC : 检查脉搏，进行胸外按压
uD : 对室颤/无脉搏室速病人进行评估及给予除颤治疗
The first steps are the same when you respond to any potential cardiorespiratory emergency:
CAssess rescuer and victim safety.
CAssess responsiveness.
C“Phone fast.”
CPosition the victim.
CPosition yourself as the rescuer.
 Primary ABCD Survey
CCheck the ABCs.
CPerform CPR until the defibrillator is attached for defibrillation.
CAssess the rhythm, hunting for 室颤.
CDeliver defibrillatory shocks in close sequence: shock 1: 200 J; shock 2: 200 to 300 J; shock 3: up to 360 J if 室颤 is persistent.
