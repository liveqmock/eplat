The Secondary ABCD Survey for the 室颤/Pulseless 室速 Algorithm is, for treatment purposes, the same for all 3 cardiac arrest algorithms: advanced airway management, effective ventilation, access to the bloodstream, and administration of rhythm-appropriate medications.
The effective team leader will have already assigned 4 tasks at the start of resuscitation:
CA rescuer to perform chest compressions
CA rescuer to manage the airway, including ventilation adjuncts and advanced airway control
CA rescuer to establish IV or IO access for medication administration
