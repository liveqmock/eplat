It is critical that ACLS provider candidates understand the difference between pacing artifact, pacing without QRS capture, and pacing with QRS capture.
Also note electrical capture vs mechanical capture. If pacing produces the spike and broad QRS complexes seen in the third rhythm strip (electrical capture), you must check to see whether the paced beats are producing a detectable pulse and blood pressure.
